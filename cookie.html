Guidelines for the use of cookies

The protection of personal data is very important to us. Our highest priority is dealing with it in a trustworthy and transparent way. So that you understand what that means for you, below you will find a detailed explanation about what cookies are and what the cookie policy is at V&S Brothers Inc. (hereafter V&S Brothers). Additionally, we explain how you can restrict or prevent the use of cookies.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that the browser stores on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet, etc.) when you visit a website. Once you visit the V&S Brothers website or use another service provided by V&S Brothers, information is collected and stored through cookies. The advantage of cookies for you is that it can improve your browsing experience, because your habits and needs can be identified. Cookies help to provide, keep up to date, and constantly improve services.

There are several types of cookies that you can read about in more detail below.

Types of cookies used on the V&S Brothers website:

Cookies are classified according to origin, purpose, or expiration date. The cookies used by V&S Brothers serve different purposes; their validity periods vary.

  • Origin

    • First-party cookies: deposited and managed directly by V&S Brothers.

    Third-party cookies: deposited and managed by partners to perform statistical analysis about how users navigate the V&S Brothers website. This analysis is performed completely anonymously.

  • Purpose

    • Technical cookies: ensure, among other things, smooth navigation on the website. In addition, they allow the use of certain tools with restricted access.

    Cookies for analysis purposes: allow statistics about website visitors to be collected, such as the number of visitors to different areas of the website, frequency of visits, or user behavior and habits. The navigation, services, offers, as well as important notifications on our website can be improved.

  • Validity

    • Session cookies: these cookies collect and store data for the duration of the user's visit to the V&S Brothers website.

    Persistent cookies: these cookies collect and store data in the browser within a variable period of time, depending on the purpose.

    Special browser settings allow you to accept, block, or disable all or some cookies on your device.

Even if your browser settings have been changed to block certain cookies, you can still access information from the entire V&S Brothers website. However, the operation or use of certain services offered by V&S Brothers may be limited.

If you have consented to the use of cookies, you can withdraw this consent at any time. Existing cookies will be deleted.

V&S Brothers reserves the right to change this cookie policy if changes are made to the configuration and/or use of cookies. An updated version will be published on this website.
