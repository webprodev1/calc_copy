International Shipping

You buy the vehicle and we’ll look after getting it to you!

HomeServicesInternational Shipping

We have extensive experience in exporting vehicles from the US and Canada worldwide. Whether you have purchased your vehicle privately or through V&S Brothers, once your vehicle arrives at our exit port, we will look after everything from preparing the proper export documentation for Customs to determining the most suitable and economical way to ship your vehicle to your country.

Our advanced and frequently updated shipping calculator allows you to check the cost of shipping a vehicle to your country. If, in the rare case, your country or vehicle type does not appear on our shipping calculator, please do not hesitate to contact us for a quote.

We work closely with all major shipping lines, such as Maersk, MSC, CMA CGM, COSCO, Evergreen Marine, Hapag-Lloyd, Hamburg Süd, Hanjin Shipping, OOCL and UASC. For this reason, we are able to organise shipments to all corners of the globe.

We offer the RORO and Container options for shipping. In addition, where possible, we will coordinate consolidation of your shipment so that your costs are kept to a minimum. We have acquired substantial expertise in all the logistical aspects of international shipping, so you can be assured that we will determine the very best option for you.

All of our US exit ports are equipped with forklifts and cranes, so even if the purchased car has 4 flat tires, it will be loaded into the container for export without a problem. The same applies for heavy equipment and other cargo.

In addition to the export of all kinds of vehicles, we are also able to coordinate the export of heavy equipment or commercial cargo. Again, we will use our vast logistics expertise to ensure your goods are delivered safely and promptly to your country. We are confident that we will be able to use our experience in this field to provide you with the very best option to deliver your merchandise.

If commercial cargo that is not on skids/pallets is delivered to our warehouses , we will pelletize it before shipping. Customized pallet and wooden casing options are also available.

Please do not hesitate to contact us to discuss how we might be able to get your goods to you.
