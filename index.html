    F.A.Q

    Who can buy a car from us?

    Use of our car-related services is restricted to international and US dealers. You must buy and/or ship at least 10 vehicles per month to become and stay an active user of V&S Brothers.

    If your business operates from outside of North America and you’d like to purchase/ship a car through V&S Brothers, no dealer licence is required. In such a case, all purchased vehicles must be exported outside of the USA and Canada.

    Is registration required?

    Yes. Fill out all required information on the registration page. Please note that you will need to upload a copy of your passport or business license before you can obtain full access to the V&S Brothers website and services.

    Does information about my clients remain private?

    Yes, your information will be kept safe on our SSL secured server.

    Information about your clients will not be published anywhere on our website. Please read our Privacy Policy for more information.

    Does information about vehicles I bought and or ship using your services remain private?

    Yes, your information will be kept safe on our SSL secured server.

    Information about your purchases will not be published anywhere on our website with the exception of the “My account” section of our website, which is password protected and accessible only by you and your authorized users.

    Please read our Privacy Policy for more information.

    What if I register, but buy/ship less cars per month than required?

    If you buy less than 10 cars per month, you are required to pay a surcharge of $200/vehicle below the pre-requisite number of vehicles to be purchased per month.

    If you have simply had a slow month, no problem - we’ll waive this charge. However, please note, the surcharge can only be waived no more than 3 times per year, where the year commences on the date of your account registration.

    What identification documents are accepted?

    Any government issued ID, such as passport or any other major identification document. You may upload a copy of your ID during registration or after your registration request has been approved and you have access to your V&S Brothers account.

    Is V&S Brothers only for international buyers?

    No, American and Canadian businesses are more than welcome to use our services. As a matter of fact, many American dealerships outsource their shipping for their international sales to us. American wholesale companies also find our shipping rates very attractive for their global sales.
